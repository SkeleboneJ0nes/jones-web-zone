Conway's Game of Life

    This is the simplest version of Conway's Game of Life that you will make during our session. This simulation follows a few simple rules:

  1. A cell with 1 or 0 neighbors will die, as if of loneliness.
  2. A cell with 4 or more neighbors will die, as if from overpopulation.
  3. A cell with 2 or 3 neighbors happily survives :)
  4. If an empty cell has 3 neighbors, a new cell is born!
