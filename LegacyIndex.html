Title Screen Gameplay (click to play!) Description Notes
Play as a mouse to escape the maze! Collect cheese to get a hi-score, but avoid moldy cheese and mouse traps or you'll get a game over! Can you escape the maze with the highest score possible? This project was my favorite from 1st quarter 2023! It was created by 8th graders Audreina S. and Anna S. The project is based on the 80s Rockstar Maze skillmap - they took the code and expanded to give it multiple levels and custom art/music.
Race past the opposition and try for a hi-scoring distance! Hi score stored between play sessions, so you can always keep track of your best score. Avoid driving off the road or bumping into other racers, or you will lose health! This was a technically advanced and well-polished project submitted by 7th graders in 4th quarter (2024). Carter K. is responsible for the majority of the coding on this project, and it's clear to see he put a lot of effort into it! His group members Roman K. and Nikko C. helped with planning. I find it very impressive that they were able to create a working hi score system.
